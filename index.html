About Us

Have you ever heard of Mario and Luigi brothers ?
That's us !
You may be wondering how we ended up on a circus, let me tell you a story.

You see back in the day, I was busy running around, jumping through obstacles and facing all sort of creatures just to rescue someone, who for whatever reason kept getting kidnapped.

I felt like I was being scammed, so I decided to take my destiny in my own hands and run the show !

And so began the Wild Circus !

Come and witness the truly unique and exceptionnal experience we have to offer !
